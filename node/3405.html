Licensing Forms & Instructions

Any change to information provided in order to obtain a contractors license must be provided to the Registrar of Contractors within 30 calendar days of the change.

Click to open/download the form

  • Update Address, Phone and Email Info RC-L-302
    Use this form to change the company mailing address, this must be done within 30 calendar days of the move. Mail original form with original signature to P.O. Box 6688, Phoenix, AZ 85005-6688.
  • Assumption RC-L-242
    Use this form to assume responsibility of any license being canceled when applying for a new license. One (1) copy is required for each license being applied for, to be sent to any office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors.
  • Bond Form RC-L-220D
    You may furnish this form to your insurance company, licensed to write bonds in the State of Arizona. See bond instructions below.

    Sample Bonds:
    • Bond Example for a Sole Proprietor
    • Bond Example for an Entity With DBA
    • Bond Example for an Entity Without DBA
  • Bond Instructions RC-L-220I
    You may wish to read these instructions before acquiring a license bond.
  • License Cancellation Form RC-L-201A
    Use this form to cancel (terminate) a license. One (1) copy may be sent to any office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors, that single copy may list all of the licenses you wish to cancel. A canceled license can not be reactivated.
  • Exemption from Qualifying Party Requirements Application RC-L-280
    Use this form to apply for a qualifying party exemption. Each license requires a separate form and fees. Be sure you meet all of these requirements before applying for an exemption.
  • License Inactivation Form RC-L-201B
    Use this form to place a license inactive. One (1) completed form is required for each license to be placed in inactive status. Inactivating a license keeps it from expiring during a period when it is not intended to be used. It can then be reinstated within five (5) years of the inactivation date.
  • License Classification Change Form RC-L-201C
    Use this form to opt-out of a dual license classification issued to your business.
  • Name Change Request RC-L-300
    Use this form if you wish to change the company name listed on your license, one (1) copy is required to be sent to any office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors.
  • Name Change Request for Certificates of Deposit RC-L-293
    If a Certificate of Deposit is held in lieu of the surety bond, include this form instead of a bond rider. Please include one completed form for each license number.
  • Application for New Qualifying Party RC-L-201D
    Use this form for replacing the qualified party of any license. One (1) copy is required for each license the qualifying party is being changed on, to be sent to any office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors.
  • Notice of Disassociation/Resignation RC-L-305
    Use this form to report the resignation of a qualifying party. Any officer, partner or member of a limited liability company. One (1) copy is required to be sent to the Phoenix office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors.
  • Personnel Change Form; RC-L-286
    Use this form to report any change of personnel or change in the percentage of ownership. One (1) copy is required to be sent to any office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors.
  • Reinstatement Packet RC-L-222
    Use this form to reinstate a license that has been placed on inactive status. One (1) copy is required for each license to be reinstated, and the Arizona Registrar of Contractors must have 30 days notice before the license is due to expire.
  • Request for Affidavit RC-L-303
    Use this form to request an affidavit with regards to information concerning any license. One (1) copy is required, to be sent to any office of the Arizona Registrar of Contractors with a $10.00 processing fee.
  • Workers Comp RC-L-317A
    Use this form to show proof of compliance with the workers compensation act. One (1) copy is required to obtain or renew a contractors license.
  • Petition for Review of Criminal Record Form RC-L-100
    Use of this form is not required for application but enables the agency to make a determination whether the applicant's criminal history would preclude licensure prior to submitting an application.
  • Scope Determination Request Form RC-L-203
    After researching our existing license classifications here, if you're still not sure what license you need, you may use this form to request a license classification determination from the Registrar.
