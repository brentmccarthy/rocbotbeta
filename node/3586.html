Anonymous Functions

You are allowed to perform certain functions in the ROC Customer Portal without having to login.

  • All activity on this website monitored and your IP address will be recorded when you submit any forms.
  • You will not be required to give your name and/or contact information if you'd prefer to remain anonymous.
  • All forms must be completed during a single session. By not logging in, you will not be able to come back and complete your form at another time so please make sure you have the requisite time needed to fill out the forms before you begin.
  • Only certain functions of our customer portal are available to anonymous users:
    1. Unlicensed Contractor Complaint Forms
    2. Contractor Search
    3. Pending Complaint and Application Status Forms (You will need to provide the pending complaint or application number to see the status message.)
